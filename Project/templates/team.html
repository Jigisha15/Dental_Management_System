Dr.Aniruddha Kajale
Periodontist & Implantologist

Periodontist of The Team Dental is a very focused and hard working dentist. Dr. Kajale has achieved his masters in periodontics from Bapuji Dental College, Davangere. Beyond his masters degree, he was associated with D. Y. Patil Dental School, Lohegaon as an assistant professor for 5 years. As a periodontist, his main focus is on management of deficient bone and soft tissue conditions in the field of periodontology and implantology. Dr. Kajale’s expertise also adds another feather to his hat as he has undergone advanced training in guided implant surgery from the DIO Digital Academy, Busan, Korea and keeps attending various continuing education courses to update his knowledge. Currently, we are proud to have a consultant who helps many private practices in Pune and in Mumbai in our team.
